    Benifits of PG

    Ensuring a safe and secure environment, Indian parents are known for their extra possessiveness for their children. If the child is a girl, their anxieties reach another level. They always want to make sure their child is safe and secure. The atmosphere in PG provides a level of satisfaction to the parents as it is well equipped with CCTV cameras, watchmen and security guards. Some PGs even make sure the person living follows proper guidelines to ensure safety. In an emergency, the PG also has access to doctors and first aid kits.


    #Freedom

    Most PG stays do not follow strict curfew rules; therefore, you won't have to compromise with your freedom. You can even stay for a night at your friend's place. All you have to do is enter the register and mention the checkout and check-in time.

    Hostel vs PG: which is better?

    The added liberty at a PG makes it a better option for accommodation for students. PGs have rules, but they are far more flexible as compared to hostels. However, PGs require a student to do a lot of their stuff by themselves. So, it helps a student become self-reliant and learn how to live independently. Also, it teaches you how you can strike a balance between your professional or college life while completing your daily tasks Considering PG or hostel depends on the choice of a student. Going by the trend, hostels are not preferred due to low maintenance of the premises, the food quality and restrictions that make the life of a student tougher. Therefore, PGs have appeared as the most preferred accommodation option.
